How It Works

1v1 Peer-to-Peer Battles

Users make sports predictions and are automatically matched with an opponent. The user who scores more points wins the pot. BattlePicks facilitates the match and takes a 5% rake — we never play against our users.

STEP 01

Choose Entry

User selects their entry amount and currency. Available currencies depend on state of residence.

STEP 02

Make Prediction

User selects a sports prediction — game outcomes (sweepstakes) or player props (DFS). Riskier picks earn more points.

STEP 03

Auto-Match

System automatically matches with another user at the same entry level with a different prediction. No opponent selection — fully random.

STEP 04

Settlement

Games finish, picks are scored by risk-based points. Higher total points wins. Winner receives the pot minus 5% platform rake.

Platform Models

Two Legal Frameworks, One Platform

BattlePicks operates under two distinct legal models with state-level availability controls. A free-play mode is available in all states.

Daily Fantasy Sports

USD

Skill-based DFS pick'em contest model. Users enter with real USD and compete by predicting individual player statistical performances. Minimum 3 picks from 2 different teams. Classified as a game of skill under UIGEA and state DFS laws.

  • PurchaseDirect entry fee

  • Format1v1 Pick'em: min 3 picks, min 2 teams

Scoring System

Risk-Based Point Scoring

Points are determined by the payout multiplier of each pick. Riskier predictions earn more points if correct. This creates a strategic layer — users balance risk and reward to outscore their opponent.

Safe Picks

Favorites (-150 to -250) earn fewer points (1.4 – 1.7) but are more likely to hit.

Risky Picks

Underdogs (+150 to +500) earn more points (2.5 – 6.0) but are less likely to hit.

Game Theory

No Nash equilibrium — the optimal strategy constantly evolves. Users balance risk vs. reward to outscore opponents.

Key Facts

Platform Overview

Revenue Model

5% rake on every completed match. Platform is a neutral facilitator — no house edge, no positions against users, no odds-setting.

Odds Source

All odds sourced from The Odds API, which aggregates from licensed US sportsbooks. BattlePicks does not create or modify odds.

Matching

Fully automated and random. Users cannot choose opponents. Entries matched by format, currency, and entry amount only. Prevents collusion.

Supported Sports

NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, NCAAB, NCAAF at launch. Additional sports to be added based on user demand and data availability.

Deposit Limits

Min: $5 (card) / $25 (ACH). Max: $250 at launch. Conservative limits to minimize fraud risk and chargebacks.

Entity

BattlePicks LLC. Registered in Texas (July 2025). Single-member LLC. Operating from California.
